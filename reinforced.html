🔄 The Reinforcement Learning Loop

Reinforcement Learning (RL) is a branch of machine learning where an AI learns by interacting with its environment through trial and error. Here are the core terms that define this interactive loop:

🕵️ Agent

The decision-maker. In our game, the Detective 👮 acts as the Agent, learning how to make the best moves.

🏙️ Environment

The world the Agent interacts with. Here, it is the City Grid Layout filled with streets, thieves, and holes.

📍 State ($S$)

The current situation of the Agent. In this grid, it represents the specific coordinates $(x, y)$ of the Detective.

⚡ Action ($A$)

The moves the Agent can make. Our Detective can choose from 4 Actions: UP, DOWN, LEFT, or RIGHT.

🎁 Reward ($R$)

The feedback from the Environment. The Detective receives a Score penalty for steps/holes, and a positive reward for the loot.

🗺️ Policy ($\pi$)

The strategy. It is the learned brain roadmap mapping each State to the best possible Action to reach the Goal.
