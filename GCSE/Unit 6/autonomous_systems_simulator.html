Key Concepts

  • Sensor: Reads physical properties (analog) or states (digital) from the real world.
  • ADC: Analog-to-Digital Converter. Microprocessors only understand 1s and 0s. The ADC converts an analog wave into a binary number.
  • Microprocessor: The "brain". It executes programmed logic based on digital inputs.
  • Actuator: Converts digital commands back into physical action (movement, heat, light).
