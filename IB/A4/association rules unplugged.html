Marketing Challenge! 🎯

Step 1: Find a Popular Pair 🕵️‍♀️

Lay out all 50 customer cards and look for two items that are frequently bought together. (Example: Coffee & Muffin).

Why? A popular pair means more customers will want your deal!

Step 2: Check the Strength 💪

  • Count how many of the 50 customers bought both items together.
  • Gather all cards with your first item. From that group, how many also have the second item?

Why? This tells you how persuasive your deal will be!

Step 3: Make Your Recommendation 💡

Based on your findings, what is the best promotional deal to offer? Explain why it's the smartest way to use the marketing budget.
