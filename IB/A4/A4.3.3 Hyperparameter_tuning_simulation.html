Concepts

  • Hyperparameter Tuning: Adjusting settings (like 'complexity' here) *before* training to find the best model architecture.
  • Underfitting (Left): Model is too simple. It performs poorly on both training and unseen (validation) data. High bias.
  • Optimal Fit (Center): Model captures the true underlying pattern without learning the noise. Good performance on both.
  • Overfitting (Right): Model is too complex. It memorizes the training data perfectly (learning the noise) but fails on new validation data. High variance.
