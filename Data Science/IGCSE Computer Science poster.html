Your Future in Tech: Skills & Pathways

Computational thinking isn't just for coders. It's a powerful way to solve problems, analyze data, and manage complex projects in **any** field. These skills are highly valued everywhere.

  • **Software Developer:** Build the apps, websites, and systems we use every day.
  • **Data Scientist:** Make sense of huge datasets to inform decisions.
  • **Cybersecurity Expert:** Protect critical information and systems from threats.
  • **Game Developer:** Design and create engaging interactive experiences.
  • **IT Consultant:** Lead technology projects and advise businesses on digital solutions.
  • **Researcher:** Innovate and contribute to the next generation of computing.
  • **Medicine & Biology:** Analyze genetic data, model diseases, and power diagnostic tools.
  • **Art & Design:** Create generative art, design 3D models, and build interactive experiences.
  • **Business & Finance:** Model financial markets, automate tasks, and analyze consumer data.
  • **Law & Journalism:** Sift through document archives and find patterns in data.
